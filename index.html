Simple omellete Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal,classic omellete combines between egss,
cooked to perfection,optionally fillled with your choice of cheese,vegetables or meats

Preparation time
  • Total
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

    • Ingredients

    • 2-3 large eggs
    • salt,to taste
    • pepper, to taste
    • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
    • optional fillings:cheese diced vegetables,cooked meats, herbs

    Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are
      well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
    2. Heat the pan:Place a non sticky frying pan over a medium heat and add butter or oil
    3. Cook the omellete:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan
      to make sure the eggs evenly coats the surface
    4. Add fillings(optional):When the eggs begins to set at the edges but are still
      runny in the middle sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omellete.
    5. Fold and serve:As the omellete continues to cook,carefully lift one edge
      and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide into plate.
    6. Enjoy:serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
